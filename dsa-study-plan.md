# DSA Interview Prep Plan - Striver's SDE Sheet

## Overview
- **Level:** Beginner
- **Daily Time:** 1-2 hours
- **Timeline:** 5-6 months (24 weeks)
- **Language:** Python
- **Goal:** Mid-tier + Top companies
- **Total Problems:** ~230 curated problems

- **Problems:** https://takeuforward.org/interviews/strivers-sde-sheet-top-coding-interview-problems/

Focus: Solve Blind 75 problems, do 2 mock interviews per week

### Week 23: Company-Tagged Problems
- Solve problems from your target companies
- Focus on Amazon, Google, Meta frequently asked topics
- Practice system design basics

### Week 24: Final Review
- Revise all weak areas
- Practice explaining solutions out loud
- Behavioral interview preparation
